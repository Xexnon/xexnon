About XEXNON

Over 600 million people still live without access to electricity, 110 million of the 600 million live in urban areas. Most who have access to electricity cannot fully rely on the grid as the grid supply constantly fails. Most people end up buying generators contributing heavily to carbon emissions. Renewable energy and efficient storage systems might be the only viable solution. The transition to renewable energy sources in today’s power systems is becoming one of the largest contributions in tackling climate change.

The significant increase in the deployment of wind and solar power and other forms of clean energy, however, requires different forms of flexibility to manage the intermittency of these sources. Due to its fast response, ease of deployment and current trends in cost reductions, battery storage is becoming a significant solution to provide this flexibility. Xexnon technology is a renewable energy storage company and service provider, using cutting edge technology to develop innovative and intelligent energy storage solutions that are accelerating the worldwide transition to sustainable and reliable energy. In keeping with our mission to provide clean energy solutions that benefit people, business, and the planet we also provide a pay-as-you-go, clean, cost effective and sustainable electricity using micro grids for clustered rural, urban, commercial and industrial sectors. We believe that affordable, clean, reliable power should be available to everyone, everywhere—from remote island villages to busy urban centers, from small commercial interests to large industrial companies.
